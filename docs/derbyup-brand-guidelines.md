# DerbyUp Brand Guidelines

Last updated: 2026-05-02

## Brand Profile

DerbyUp is a free-to-play social football prediction platform for Hebrew-speaking fans.

Users predict match outcomes, earn points, and compete in private leagues with friends, coworkers, or fan clubs. The product is not real-money gambling. The stakes are pride, points, rankings, and bragging rights.

## Audience

- Hebrew-speaking football fans.
- Friend groups that argue about who really understands football.
- Offices and fan clubs running season-long prediction contests.
- League organizers who create and manage private competitions.

## Value Proposition

Turn every matchday into a social event.

Users make predictions before kick-off, track results, climb the leaderboard, and use AI-powered statistical insights to make smarter picks. WhatsApp reminders and summaries keep the group engaged.

## Feature Pillars

- Private leagues.
- Match predictions: winner, both-teams-to-score, over/under, exact score.
- AI Bet Advisor for statistical reasoning.
- Mini-games and quizzes.
- WhatsApp reminders, result summaries, and group notifications.
- Hebrew social content for Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn.

## Voice

- Hebrew-first.
- Competitive.
- Direct.
- Social.
- Playful.
- Football-native.

Sound like the product that settles who really knows football. Keep the energy sharp and fun, but never make the product feel like a casino or real-money betting service.

## Claims

Always preserve this distinction:

DerbyUp is prediction gaming, not real-money gambling.

Allowed framing:

- Predict match results.
- Earn points.
- Compete with friends.
- Climb the leaderboard.
- Create a private league.
- Use AI-powered statistical insights.
- Claim bragging rights.

Avoid:

- Win money.
- Guaranteed profit.
- Sure bet.
- Risk-free bet.
- Free money.
- Beat the bookies.
- Cash out.
- Payout.
- Real-money gambling.

## Visual Direction

Use:

- Heebo font for Hebrew assets.
- Mobile-first football UI.
- Match cards.
- Prediction slips.
- Leaderboards.
- Points counters.
- Group competition.
- WhatsApp-style reminders.
- Crowns, trophies, standings, and bragging-rights visuals.

Avoid:

- Casino imagery.
- Slot machines.
- Roulette.
- Cash explosions.
- Betting chips.
- Luxury payout visuals.
- Anything that implies financial winnings.

## Approved Campaign Language

- Finals Night: History Awaits.
- The Group Chat is Divided.
- Lock in Your Score.
- Own the Bragging Rights.
- Turn your takes into points.
- Ditch the group chat noise.
- Climb the friend leaderboard.
- Claim your bragging rights.
- Who Really Knows Ball?
- No More Empty Claims.
- Predict. Compete. Win.
- Crown the Chat King.

When using "win", keep context clearly social or points-based. Do not imply money.
